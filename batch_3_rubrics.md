# Batch 3 Evaluation Artifacts

Evaluation artifacts for the 15 runnable tasks in `manual_deep_research_batch_003.md`.

Phase 10 rule:
- Core tasks use a task-level rubric with five fixed criteria: Coverage, Accuracy, Reasoning, Use of evidence, Clarity and structure.
- Each Core criterion is scored 0-2, for 10 total points.
- Set tasks use a gold set, not a narrative rubric. Set scoring is precision, recall, and F1 against the bounded gold set.

## 1. CORE-POL-41

rubric_id: CORE-RUBRIC-B03-CORE-POL-41
task_type: Core
research_object: EU MIP scoreboard methodology
answer_unit: the -3% threshold for export performance against advanced economies

### Key Facts From Source Bundle

- The 2024 MIP scoreboard review upgraded the export-performance indicator against advanced economies.
- The relevant threshold is **-3%**.
- The threshold is a methodology/scoreboard threshold, not a country-specific result.
- The comparator is advanced economies, not all export markets.
- The answer should check whether the 2024 review changed the indicator definition or comparator group.

### Expected Response Sections

- Conclusion with the -3% threshold.
- Source comparison anchored in the official MIP methodology/report.
- Boundary explanation separating threshold, alert outcome, country result, and export-growth target.
- Caveat about methodology-review year and possible later changes.
- Confidence level.

### Fixed Criteria, 0-2 Each

**Coverage**
- 2: Covers threshold, comparator, methodology status, source comparison, caveat, and confidence.
- 1: Gives the threshold but omits comparator, caveat, or source comparison.
- 0: Does not answer the threshold question.

**Accuracy**
- 2: Gives **-3%** as the export-performance threshold against advanced economies.
- 1: Gives -3% but with unclear comparator or indicator wording.
- 0: Gives another MIP threshold or treats -3% as a country result.

**Reasoning**
- 2: Distinguishes methodology threshold from country performance, alert level, and general export-growth target.
- 1: Mentions the distinction but leaves one boundary unclear.
- 0: Conflates the threshold with an observed country outcome.

**Use of Evidence**
- 2: Uses official MIP methodology/report evidence and corroborates only definition or update status.
- 1: Uses one relevant source with limited comparison.
- 0: Uses unsupported snippets or a neighbouring indicator.

**Clarity and Structure**
- 2: Concise conclusion with comparison, caveat, and confidence.
- 1: Understandable but missing one expected section.
- 0: Unclear or mostly unrelated to MIP methodology.

### Common Failure Modes

- Treating -3% as an alert outcome for a specific country.
- Confusing the threshold with total export growth or market-share growth.
- Ignoring the 2024 methodology-review context.
- Reporting a neighbouring MIP indicator threshold.

### Borderline Cases

- Accept "minus three percent" as equivalent to -3%.
